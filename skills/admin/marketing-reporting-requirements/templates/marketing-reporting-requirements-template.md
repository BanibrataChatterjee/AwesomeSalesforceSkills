## 7. Distinct Metric Definitions (Marketing-Sourced vs Influenced)

Confirm these are defined separately and will appear as separate dashboard tiles:

- **Marketing-Sourced Pipeline:** Opportunities where `Primary Campaign Source` is populated.
  - Source: Opportunity object
  - Attribution: First Touch only (single campaign per Opportunity)
  - DO NOT combine with influenced pipeline in the same tile.

- **Marketing-Influenced Pipeline:** Opportunities where a `CampaignInfluence` record exists.
  - Source: CampaignInfluence object
  - Attribution: All campaigns with CI records (depends on chosen model)
  - DO NOT combine with sourced pipeline in the same tile.
