# Gotchas — OmniStudio Multi-Language

## Gotcha 1: LDJ out of sync

**What happens:** Missing translations after script edit.

**When it occurs:** Forgot to re-export.

**How to avoid:** Post-edit: re-export + diff.


---

## Gotcha 2: Hard-coded strings in FlexCard

**What happens:** Stay English after translation.

**When it occurs:** Static text used.

**How to avoid:** Labels everywhere.


---

## Gotcha 3: RTL broken

**What happens:** Layout reversed wrong.

**When it occurs:** No RTL testing.

**How to avoid:** Preview in Hebrew/Arabic explicitly.
